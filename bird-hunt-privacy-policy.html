Privacy Policy for Bird Hunt

Last updated: 31.10.2024

Thank you for choosing to play Bird Hunt! Your privacy is extremely important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding it.

1. No Data Collection

Bird Hunt does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. The game does not require, request, or access any form of data, such as personal identifiers, location, or usage statistics. No data is shared with third parties or retained on any server, as the game operates entirely offline.

2. No Internet Connection Required

Bird Hunt is an offline game and does not require an internet connection to play. As such, the game will not send or receive any information over the internet at any time.

3. No Third-Party Services

Bird Hunt does not use any third-party services, such as analytics providers, advertising networks, or social media integrations. This further ensures that no data is shared, processed, or accessed outside of the game.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Although Bird Hunt does not collect or use any data, we may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. If we make any changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
